      Hotels in Shimla
      Shimla offers an endearing combination of snow-covered peaks, clear blue sky, green pines, woods and vibrant roofs which have made it one of the most popular tourist destinations of India. Apart from the stunning landscapes, the city encloses buildings with colonial architecture depicting the history of India. This beautiful hill station is also a home to educational institutions, museums, religious temples and churches that attract tourists in large number throughout the year. The high number of tourist footfall can also be gauged by the presence of numerous hotels in Shimla, the city offers a range of accommodations varying from luxury hotels to hostels, lavish resorts to comfortable homestays and cottages. Goibibo is one such platform that will bring forth all the best options for you at one place and let you book the most suitable one with the best deals and offers.

      For a luxurious stay you can choose among the 5 star hotels in Shimla, Radisson Jass, Marina -Shimla First Designer Boutique Hotel, The Oberoi Cecil, Clarkes Hotel and some others. Other than the 5 star hotels, you can also opt for staying at resorts in Shimla. Snow Valley Resort, Koti Resorts, Kamna Hill Resorts, Marigold Sarovar Portico and several others. These hotels and resorts are equipped with the amenities that can provide you with a lavish stay and delight you with quality services offered by them.

      Usually, the tourists prefer to stay around the Mall Road, which lies at the heart of the city and has an abundance of hotels, shops, cafes and restaurants. Hotel Shingar, Hotel Silverline, Hotel Willow Banks, Hotel Combermere are some of the good hotels in Shimla Mall Road.

      When these top-notch options fall heavy on your pocket, you can look out for some budget hotels in Shimla that can suit your budget. Hotel Sansar, Auckland Hotel, Hotel White, Hotel Sunrise, Hotel Dreamland and several others can serve your purpose rightly. Another best option for your accommodation can be homestays and cute wooden cottages in the city.

      You can expect a calm environment, beautiful gardens, spacious rooms and great mountain views while choosing homestays in Shimla as your accommodation. To name a few homestays that can be your preference to include are Verma Homestay, Kufri Greens Homestay, L R Heights Homestay, Ridge View Homestay etc. Also, Wood Vista, Pine Cottage Estate by Boho, Alpine Stone Cottage, Cedar Cottage are some of the cottages in Shimla that can be chosen as per your preferences. You can read customer reviews and ratings, check the pictures of the property on the Goibibo App and book your accommodation through it easily.

      While heading out to explore the charm of Shimla, some of the must-visit tourist spots for you can be Jakhoo Hill & Temple which will serve a dual purpose of experiencing an adventurous trek and worshipping the historic Hanuman statue, The Scandal Point Ridge to capture and witness spectacular sunrise and sunset, Annadale for golf lovers, Naldehra and Shaily peak, Chadwick Falls and the famous Mall Road. The Shimla State Museum, Christ Church, Viceregal Lodge are some of the captivating historical buildings that you can visit.

      Shopping is invariably enjoyed by the tourists and Shimla has markets such as Tibetan Market, Lakkar Bazar and Mall Road from where you can shop for Himachal handicraft products, woven carpets, hats, woollen wears, wooden home pieces and some beautiful jewellery. One of the best ways to experience the local culture of any place you visit is to savour the local dishes. On your trip to Shimla, you can try the Himachali cuisine and some popular dishes such as Channa Madra, Siddu, Sepu Badi Madra and others too.

      With the ease of hotel booking through the Goibibo App, you are halfway through having a peaceful trip to Shimla.
